Our Services

Security Audit

Our comprehensive security audits are tailored for individuals and small businesses. We assess your existing digital infrastructure, perform vulnerability scans, and deliver a detailed report with actionable recommendations to strengthen your defences against modern threats.

Cyber Awareness Training

Participate in our interactive and up-to-date training sessions designed to educate non-tech-savvy users on recognising suspicious emails, understanding secure password practices, and implementing multi-factor authentication so you can fend off cyberattacks confidently.

Ongoing Support

We provide continuous cybersecurity monitoring and timely technical support. Whether it’s troubleshooting issues, patching vulnerabilities, or ensuring your software is current, our team is available to deliver prompt assistance and maintain optimal digital security.

Consulting

Leverage our expert consulting services for bespoke cybersecurity strategies. From one-off consultations to ongoing strategic advice, we work with you to implement robust security measures that blend seamlessly with your business operations.

About Us

We are a local cybersecurity consultancy passionate about helping the South West stay secure. Our focus is on simplicity, affordability, and building trust with non-tech-savvy users and small businesses.
